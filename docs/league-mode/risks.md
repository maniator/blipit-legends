## Aggregate posture

- **High-severity risks** cluster in v1 (foundation) and v3 (trades + playoffs). They are all mitigated by design choices already in this doc set. Any further mitigation is owed only as PR-level test coverage.
- **No critical risks remain unmitigated** at the planning layer.
- **Doc-count budget** (#1) is the single risk most likely to bite us in v4 and warrants a measurement task before v4 starts.
- **Roster-edit lock** (#6) is new and needs both a storage-layer guard and a route-level UX surface — both are specified, and the storage-layer guard is the binding contract.
